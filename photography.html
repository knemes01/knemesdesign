Photography

Kristen discovered her love for photography during the summer of 2015. Though she had always had a fascination with the camera, she really only had the chance to fully embrace it in her first college photography course where she studied the basics of traditional black and white film photography. From there she moved on to digital photography, experimentation with color editing and creating compositions.
