Projects 2016

2016 was strongly rooted in experimentation of materials, form, and meaning. Projects such as 2000 Folds and 4: A Design Exhibition focus primarily on the material of paper, what forms it could take, and the meanings those forms could convey. Earlier projects focus more on the effect of textures and color that paper could give in design.
